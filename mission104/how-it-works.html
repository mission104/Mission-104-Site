How it works

No mystery pricing. That would be ironic.

My whole business is removing mystery from your infrastructure — so the process and the money work the same way. Four steps, start to finish.

Port 01 — The process

Four steps

Reach out

Email, call, or text — whatever's easiest. Tell me what's bugging you, even if it's just "we have a closet and we're afraid of it." You don't need to know the technical words. That's my department.

Free on-site assessment

I come to your business, anywhere in my South Florida service area, and look at the actual situation with my own eyes. No cost, no obligation. I'll tell you what I see in plain English while I'm standing there.

One honest quote

After the assessment you get a single, clear quote for the work. No surprise line items, no "processing fees," no upsell script. If something isn't worth fixing, I'll tell you that too — it's cheaper for both of us.

The work — labeled and documented

I do the job, clean up after myself, and leave your infrastructure labeled and mapped. The goal is that the next person who opens that closet — even if it's not me — knows exactly what they're looking at.

Port 02 — About the money

What "free assessment" actually means

Free means free: I drive out, I look, I explain what I found, and you decide. The quote covers the work itself. Most jobs are one-time projects — fix it, document it, done. If you like having me around, ask about ongoing support; that option is coming.
